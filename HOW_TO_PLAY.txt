PLACEMENT RULES

All buildings must be connected to a road.
All buildings can not be placed directly by mines and windmills

House - costs 5 wood and 5 stone. Consumes food, "dies" if has no food left.
Pathway - costs 1 wood and 1 stone. Connects everything up.
Windmill - costs 10 wood and 5 stone. Creates farmland that grows crops.
